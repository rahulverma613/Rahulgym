THE JOURNEY
04
October 2024
DAY ONE — 52 KG
Walked into the gym at 52 kg. Started with the empty bar. No experience, no base — just the decision to show up.
Late 2024
BUILDING THE BASE
Learned the fundamental movement patterns — bench, squat, deadlift, overhead press. Focused on form over weight.
Early 2025
SINGLE MUSCLE SPLIT BEGINS
Started training with a structured 6-day single muscle split — one muscle group per day, full focus and volume per session. The foundation of real progress.
Mid 2025
FIRST MAJOR PRs HIT
Bench started climbing past 60kg. Body weight crossed 60 kg for the first time. Deadlift surpassed 120 kg.
2026 — Recent Switch
UPGRADED TO DOUBLE MUSCLE SPLIT
After mastering single-muscle training, switched to a higher-frequency double muscle split — hitting each muscle group twice a week for accelerated growth and more training variety.
June 2026 — Now
340 KG TOTAL · 65 KG · DOUBLE SPLIT
80kg bench · 100kg squat · 160kg deadlift. 13 kg added to the frame. Running the double muscle split. 20 months in — this is just the beginning.
